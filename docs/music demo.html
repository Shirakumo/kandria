Location Backstory

The camp is located in the ruins of the former city of Zenith. Zenith was situated in a valley and is surrounded by a steep mountain range, with the ocean beyond the range to the east.

In the calamity most of the city was wiped from the surface, and its remains turned the valley into a desert, with ruins and remnants of the city scattered about. Due to the harsh temperatures of the desert, and generally it being uninhabitable, it is mostly seen as a lost cause, and underground dwellers typically don't even bother going outside.

The camp is composed of a couple of somewhat intact buildings, and a sizeable piece of farmland that was meticulously cleaned of the sand, located next to a bigger chunk of ruins of the old city. One of the buildings connects to the Zenith West Crossing underground through a maintenance tunnel. Located close to the camp is a natural cave entrance that leads into another complex of underground ruins.

Game Story

The story of the game begins a few decades after the calamity. A group of people have learned the location of an old-world seed cache, containing plant seeds that can only be grown with sufficient light â€“ energy that can ill be spared in the underground settlements. Growing displeased with their home faction's militaristic tendencies, they decide to defect and try to establish a new settlement on the surface. Finding some somewhat intact buildings still remaining on the surface near the seed cache, they set up camp there and begin their efforts to grow crops.

On one of many scavenging hunts they discover the remains of an android, lying dormant underneath the rubble, deep underground. Android technology having reached an almost mythological air, one of the scavengers, Catherine, is ecstatic to find out if it still works, and starts fiddling with the controls. Almost miraculously she manages to reboot the android, who awakens, though having suffered severe damage to their long term memory.

At this point the player gains control of the android, called "The Stranger," and follows Catherine and her companion Alex back up through the ruins to the surface camp. As you arrive there's little time for introductions however, and you are immediately sent off on a mission back underground to fix some leaking pipes. Without constant water supply, the dry desert air threatens to wipe out the crops.

Throughout the game you then periodically return to the camp to take on new missions, talk to the rest of your group, and so forth.

Characters

The camp houses The Stranger, Fi, Jack, Catherine, and Alex. You can find portraits of them as well as detailed descriptions of their jobs, characters, and so forth in the characters document.
